# Quantum Mechanics and Quantum Computing Resources

A curated list of legal, free, and high-value resources for learning quantum mechanics and quantum computing.

---

## Quantum Mechanics (Fundamentals)

- The Physics of Quantum Mechanics (Binney & Skinner)  
  PDF covering quantum mechanics in depth.  
  https://www-thphys.physics.ox.ac.uk/people/JamesBinney/qb.pdf

- Quantum Mechanics for Engineers  
  Free PDF oriented for engineers.  
  https://web1.eng.famu.fsu.edu/~dommelen/quantum/pdf/index.pdf

- Modern Introductory Quantum Mechanics with Interpretation (David R. Thayer)  
  Free textbook covering quantum mechanics with interpretation angles.  
  https://www.uwyo.edu/physics/_files/docs/modern-introductory-quantum-mechanics-with-interpretation-2019.pdf

- Part QM: Quantum Mechanics (Open Textbook Library)  
  Free graduate-level textbook on quantum mechanics.  
  https://open.umn.edu/opentextbooks/textbooks/part-qm-quantum-mechanics

---

## Quantum Computing & Information

- Quantum Computing for the Quantum Curious  
  Open-access book introducing quantum computing.  
  https://library.oapen.org/handle/20.500.12657/48236

- Quantum Computer Science: An Introduction  
  Free digital book for computer scientists.  
  https://www-f1.ijs.si/~ramsak/Nanofizika/QCS/books_3092_0.pdf

- Quantum Country  
  Free interactive essay/book series on quantum computing and mechanics.  
  https://quantum.country

- FreeComputerBooks “Quantum Computing Books” Collection  
  Curated list of free textbooks on quantum computing.  
  https://www.freecomputerbooks.com/compscQuantumComputingBooks.html

---

## Specialized / Emerging Topics

- Understanding Quantum Technologies 2024  
  Free ebook on quantum technologies (hardware, software, industry, societal impact).  
  https://arxiv.org/abs/2111.15352

- Quantum Thermodynamics: An Introduction to the Thermodynamics of Quantum Information (Deffner & Campbell)  
  Free resource covering thermodynamics in quantum information.  
  https://arxiv.org/abs/1907.01596

- Quantum Computing Lecture Notes (CWI)  
  Free lecture notes covering quantum algorithms and complexity.  
  https://homepages.cwi.nl/~rdewolf/qcnotes.pdf
