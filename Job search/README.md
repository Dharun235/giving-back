# Job Search Resources

A collection of verified and trustworthy websites for job search across industries, tech, and global opportunities. Focused on authenticity and real opportunities.

---

## 1. Global & General Job Boards

- **LinkedIn Jobs** – https://www.linkedin.com/jobs : Professional networking + job listings. Highly credible.  
- **Indeed** – https://www.indeed.com : Aggregates listings from multiple sources; very widely used.  
- **Glassdoor** – https://www.glassdoor.com : Jobs + company reviews and salary insights.  
- **SimplyHired** – https://www.simplyhired.com : Aggregated job listings with filters.  
- **Monster** – https://www.monster.com : One of the oldest, reliable job boards.  

---

## 2. Tech / Developer Focused

- **Stack Overflow Jobs** – https://stackoverflow.com/jobs : Tech & software development jobs with community insights.  
- **GitHub Jobs (archived, check linked companies)** – https://jobs.github.com (some repos still maintain lists).  
- **HackerRank Job Boards** – https://www.hackerrank.com/jobs : Coding challenges + recruitment opportunities.  
- **AngelList** – https://angel.co/jobs : Startup jobs and tech roles globally.  
- **Remote OK** – https://remoteok.io : Remote tech jobs and startups.  

---

## 3. Startup & Innovation Focus

- **Wellfound (formerly AngelList Talent)** – https://wellfound.com : Startups hiring across roles.  
- **Y Combinator Startup Jobs** – https://www.workatastartup.com : Jobs at YC-backed startups.  
- **Techstars Talent** – https://www.techstars.com/jobs : Startup jobs at Techstars companies.  

---

## 4. Specialized / Niche Job Boards

- **IEEE Job Site** – https://jobs.ieee.org : Engineering, electronics, and tech jobs worldwide.  
- **USAJOBS** – https://www.usajobs.gov : Government jobs in the United States.  
- **EuroJobs** – https://www.eurojobs.com : Jobs across Europe.  
- **Jobs.ac.uk** – https://www.jobs.ac.uk : Academic, research, and science roles globally.  
- **Idealist** – https://www.idealist.org : NGO, nonprofit, and social impact roles.  

---

## 5. Career Resources & Networking

- **LinkedIn Learning** – https://www.linkedin.com/learning : Skill-building + networking for career advancement.  
- **Coursera Career Center** – https://www.coursera.org/careers : Upskilling courses + opportunities.  
- **Meetup Professional Groups** – https://www.meetup.com : Networking and local career events.  
- **Handshake** – https://joinhandshake.com : Student-focused career opportunities, internships, and entry-level jobs.  

---

> ⚠️ **Tips for Safe Job Search**:  
> - Avoid sites that ask for upfront fees for applications.  
> - Verify the company before sharing personal information.  
> - Prefer jobs listed on credible and professional platforms.  
> - Always cross-check listings across multiple sources.  

---

You are free to add more **trusted job platforms or niche boards**, but avoid unverified sources. Focus on **authenticity, credibility, and real opportunities**.
